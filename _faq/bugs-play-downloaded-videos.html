--- categories: - player - bugs type: info title: "Play downloaded videos in-app" ---

In order to play downloaded videos, you need to use an external video player.

There have been considerations implementing this directly into NewPipe. This idea was dropped due to these reasons:

  • A phone is usually shipped with a video player.
  • NewPipe is not designed to be an all-around video player.
  • A huge additional maintenance effort would be required to make this work.
  • There are other apps available that deal with offline playback, in case the phone doesn't have one.
