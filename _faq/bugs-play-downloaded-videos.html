--- categories: - player - bugs type: info title: "Playing downloaded videos" ---

To play downloaded videos a external video player is required.

There have been considerations implementing this directly into NewPipe. This idea was dropped due to these reasons:

  • Normally a phone is shipped with a video player.
  • NewPipe is not designed to be an all-around video player.
  • A huge addtional maintenance effort would be required to make this work.
  • There are already entire apps available that deal with offline playback.
